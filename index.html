Welcome to Fisherwomans world!

Fishing is a fantastic way to relax and to get out and enjoy nature. There are many beautiful places to fish within Manchester that don't cost the earth.

You don't need to have lots of expensive equipment, all you need is a basic fishing rod or pole and some bait and you're good to go. If you are not sure about what type of bait to use, your local tackle shop will be happy to help.

There are many places to go fishing within the Greater Manchester area, and a brilliant transport network makes it easy to reach many different fishing spots.

Don't forget the sandwiches and flask of coffee to keep you going!
