Chorlton Water Park

Chorlton Water Park is a Local Nature Reserve and stands on the site of Barlow Hall Farm. During the construction of the M60 motorway in the 1970's, gravel was excavated from the site. The gravel pit was subsequently flooded creating the lake that is central to the Water Park today.

Day or season tickets must be obtained from Chorlton Carp Anglers: https://chorltonca.org.uk/

Address: Chorlton Water Park, Maitland Ave, Chorlton-cum-Hardy, Manchester M21 7JJ

For further information visit: http://www.manchester.gov.uk/directory_record/89200/chorlton_water_park

Boggart Hole Clough

Boggart Hole Clough is an oasis of ancient woodland amid an urban setting surrounded by houses in north Manchester. It was designated a Local Nature Reserve in 2008 and in 2014, the lake underwent treatment to revitalise and transform the water body.

Day Prices: Adults - 2 rods: £4.00, 3 rods: £6.00. Juniors - 1 rod: £1.00

Address: Boggart Hole Clough Park, Charlestown Road, Blackley, Manchester M9 7DF

For further information visit: https://www.manchester.gov.uk/directory_record/89181/boggart_hole_clough

Heaton Park

Heaton Park is the largest park in Manchester, it is not somewhere that you would necessarily think of as a fishing venue, but there is a well established lake which will provide you with a fun day out. If you plan on heading down for a session, you need to get on the boating lake – it is not possible to fish on any of the other waters. The lake has a pretty good head of carp and is stocked with a wide range of coarse species.

Day Prices: Adults – 2 rods: £4.00, 3 rods: £6.00. Concessions – 1 rod: £1.00

Address: Heaton Park, Middleton Road, Higher Blackley, Manchester, M25 2SW

For further information visit: https://www.manchester.gov.uk/directory_record/89233/heaton_park/category/680/ponds_and_lakes

Clayton Vale

Clayton Vale is a Local Nature Reserve. Once the ash tip for east Manchester's power station, Clayton Vale was greened up in the 1980's and since recent investment, is a key green space within the Medlock River Valley. In the centre of the pond is a ring of weeds about 25m from bank which produce good carp close up using a pole.

Address: Clayton Vale, Edge Lane, Clayton, Manchester, M11 4BR

For further information visit: https://www.manchester.gov.uk/directory_record/89202/clayton_vale/category/680/ponds_and_lakes
